My Volunteering

ABOUT

Giving back to my community has always been a big part of my life. I have always made the choice to engage in the community around me in any way possible.

I was a Team Captain for Relay for Life for Alianza Latina in 2017. Being a founder and current member of the Hermanos Program, a mentorship program with Alianza Latina, has allowed me great opportunities to give back to my community.

I have volunteered at DREAM, Gavin's Foundation, Christopher's Haven, ABCD, Horizon's for Homeless Children and at local Elementary Schools in East Boston.
